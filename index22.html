Our Little Story

The Owner of Sweet Thumbs Cakes & Cupcakes is Mr. Arnold Dela Pena, Mr. Arnold Dela Pena is a culinary graduate who loves to bake cakes and cupcakes. On April 30, 2014, he decided to start a small cake shop named "Sweet Thumbs Cakes and Cupcakes" with its only branch located at San Fernando Pampanga.
